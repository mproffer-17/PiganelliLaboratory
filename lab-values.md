# Lab Values

The Piganelli Lab is committed to rigorous science, responsible research practices, collaborative training, and translational impact.

<div class="card-grid">

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Scientific Rigor</h3>
    <p>We value careful experimental design, reproducibility, thoughtful controls, and transparent interpretation of data.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Collaboration</h3>
    <p>We believe strong science is built through communication, shared expertise, and respect for diverse scientific perspectives.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Mentorship</h3>
    <p>We aim to support trainees as developing scientists, communicators, colleagues, and independent thinkers.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Responsibility</h3>
    <p>We are committed to ethical research practices, safety, compliance, and responsible use of animal and human samples.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Translational Impact</h3>
    <p>We pursue research that advances understanding of type 1 diabetes and may contribute to better prediction, prevention, or therapy.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h3>Community</h3>
    <p>We strive to create a lab environment where people can ask questions, learn from mistakes, and contribute meaningfully to the science.</p>
  </div>

</div>
